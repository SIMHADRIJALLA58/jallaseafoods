About JALLA SEA FOODS

Where the coast meets your kitchen — fresh, always.

JALLA SEA FOODS is one of the trusted seafood suppliers serving Hyderabad with premium quality fresh fishes. Our catch is transported directly from Vodarevu and Ramapuram — two of Andhra Pradesh's most-loved fishing villages.

Our main branch is located in Chirala. We ensure freshness through quick transportation, hygienic handling and timely delivery. Whether you're a restaurant, hotel, supermarket, wholesaler or household — we supply premium seafood at affordable prices.

Jalla Sea Foods is a leading supplier of fresh seafood, premium sea fish, prawns, crabs, and marine products, dedicated to delivering the highest quality catches from the ocean to your table. We work directly with trusted fishermen and coastal fishing communities to ensure every seafood product is naturally fresh, hygienically handled, and carefully packed. Our premium collection includes Black Pomfret, White Pomfret, Seer Fish (Vanjaram), Yellowfin Tuna, Skipjack Tuna, Yellowtail Kingfish, Barramundi, Grouper, Mackerel, Goatfish, Red Mullet, Tiger Prawns, White Prawns, Blue Crabs, Ribbon Fish, Silver Pomfret, and many more. Every order is processed with strict quality standards to preserve freshness, texture, flavor, and nutritional value, making Jalla Sea Foods the preferred choice for seafood lovers, restaurants, hotels, retailers, wholesalers, and seafood distributors.

Our mission at Jalla Sea Foods is to provide customers with 100% fresh fish, premium seafood, hygienically cleaned seafood, export-quality marine products, and same-day fresh catches at competitive prices. We believe that quality begins at the source, which is why every fish and seafood product undergoes careful inspection before reaching our customers. Rich in Omega-3 fatty acids, lean protein, essential vitamins, calcium, and minerals, our seafood supports a healthy lifestyle while delivering exceptional taste. Whether you are preparing traditional coastal recipes, restaurant specialties, grilled seafood, seafood curries, or premium seafood platters, our wide selection guarantees freshness, quality, and authentic ocean flavor every time.
